About Manulife

Manulife is an international leader in the financial industry aiming to help individuals make better decisions for a better life. This organization has been a strong leader in the industry for over 130 years, operating with global offices in the United States, Canada, Asia and Europe. Manulife continues to grow and push the limits by putting customers first using more digitization and innovation while fostering an inclusive and engaging company culture.

With the size and reach that Manulife operates under, the organization's integrity is always maintained by creating and prioritizing a clear mission throughout the company. Their bold mission can be supported by their values to work together and succeed as a digital, customer-focused market leader. Values like Own It, Get It Done Together and Share Your Humanity can be seen through the actions of many employees every day to create a supportive and engaging environment.

Manulife is committed to improving and finding new innovative ways to solve problems for both customers and employees. Through working in Canadian Division Technology at Manulife, I have seen the leaps they are taking through digitizing many of their projects including their Manulife Bank app becoming the number one banking app in Canada.

While working at Manulife, I had the opportunity to build on their employee experience and help bring their mission and values to life.

My Goals

Leadership

Throughout my term, one skill I wanted to improve was to ensure the development of clear, organized project plans with effective communication channels to everyone involved in these projects. Through different planning sessions, I was able to determine project requirements and develop project schedules to stay organized throughout the project. Specifically for our Security Hackathon, I developed clear instructions and was in contact with all coaches, participants and event organizers throughout the project to ensure that everyone was clear of how the event was progressing. This was also a helpful skill when it came to the development of our Co-op Award Ceremony as it allowed our team to constantly collaborate with clear ownership of different tasks and roles for the event.

Leadership

In addition to my regular day to day work tasks, I wanted to make the most of my term by getting involved in other opportunities throughout the organization. I was able to participate in multiple programs including the co-op hiring committee, co-op tech challenge and co-op HackerRank challenge. As part of the co-op hiring committee, I worked with many other full-time employees outside my immediate team to evaluate resumes and conduct interviews with prospective co-op students. Within my team, I was able to further take on the leadership role to ensure all our ideas were presented effectively and keep the project on schedule.

Personal Organization/Time Management

With the large scope of tasks and projects I wanted to get involved in throughout my term I know it would be important to ensure I am using my time effectively to prioritize and deliver high-quality work. To stay on top of my various commitments, I created a Trello board for myself to track the progress of various tasks through to-do, doing, to be approved, implementing and done. I also reviewed my project commitments and deadlines daily and weekly to adapt and structure my schedule accordingly. This system allowed me to deliver all projects on or ahead of time while ensuring the quality and creativity in each project was well above expectations.

Conclusion


I have learned so much these past few months through different leadership opportunities, working in different teams and participating in creative brainstorming sessions. One thing that made this term stand out the most was the support and connection I had from my team and Manulife as a whole. Being able to work with brilliant minds every day on my team to make real change in the organization was truly inspiring. The openness from all the Manulifers allowed me to participate in coffee chats and mentorship sessions with leaders not only in my team but throughout the organization. Building this support system while enhancing my skills has made a monumental impact as I start my career and I am so thankful for this wonderful experience.
